Hot Keys

You can use the keyboard to control many aspects of the simulation, in particular, characteristics of the stimulus.

Stimulus Hot keys
b Stimulus foreground brighter
d Stimulus foreground darker
B Background brighter
D Background darker
r Rotate stimulus
i Invert contrast of stimulus and background

UP, DOWN,

LEFT, RIGHT

 Change stimulus size
SPACE Toggle hide/show stimulus

 

General hot keys
m

Mark the tangent screen

c Clear all marks or recorded spikes
ENTER Toggle simulation running
F1 Show help
p Toggle showing photoreceptors
h Toggle hiding cell type
